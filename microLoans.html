If you only live once, what difference are you making?

Micro loans & small business development

Many times, donations that are sent for crisis relief help but only temporarily. Even if the need it met, families often remain in poverty. Micro loans are designed to establish ongoing sustainability that goes beyond relief & provides a sustainable source of income for families. Our focus is giving families who lack education, skills, and resources the opportunity to obtain the means to start an income generating business. These are simple businesses that require small initial investments and will not only provide for the family but will benefit the entire community as well.

Strategy: In order for families to receive a micro loan to start a small business, there is a process of training & accountability. The process includes...

✔ An interview and application

✔ Assessment of references & reputation by community coordinator

✔ Training & classes for skill development

✔ Mentoring, coaching, & ongoing accountability
