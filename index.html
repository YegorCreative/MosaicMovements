If you only live once, what difference are you making?

WHO WE ARE

We are a small team of entrepreneurs, leaders, and consultants focused on bringing change to East Africa regions with the greatest needs.

WHY WE STARTED

Right now as you read this, there are people in East Africa suffering. We believe we have found a solution. Our WHY comes from our belief that as human beings, it is our duty to give our resources, knowledge, and experience to help those in need.

WHAT WE DO

We invest in crisis relief, micro loans, and larger investments that solve very specific problems and sparks self-sustaining income and solutions to the needs of the communities. Through investing, consulting, and mentoring we coach local leaders and empower them to run the self-sustaining relief efforts, businesses, and projects.

WHO WE ARE

The only way to see our vision fulfilled and our mission completed is by people, like yourself, coming together and sharing our resources, time, and knowledge. If we try to do it alone, we will fail.
