Who We are

THE Origin &
Meaning of Mosaic

Mosaic art takes on a process where a large variety of different, broken pieces of glass are gathered and placed to- gether to form a beautiful masterpiece. That is the heartbeat of Mosaic Invest- ment Group. Where a large variety of different, broken human-beings gather and come together to form a beautiful master piece. Through investing in dif- ferent businesses & projects, small and large, what once was impossible can now be achieved through mosaic invest- ments and projects.

We are a small team of entrepreneurs, leaders, and consultants focused on bringing change to areas with the great- est needs. Our aim is to bring economic impact, healthcare upgrades, educa- tional improvements, and leadership de- velopment through very intentional projects and businesses.

Vision

THE Why ?

People are the most important organ- isms on planetearth. As human beings, we have a responsibility of taking care of each other. Right now, there are humans around the world dying from hunger, suffering from sickness, strug- gling to provide for family, and being crushed by poor leadership and econo- mies. We believe we have found the solution. We know that if you have the solution to help and don’t, you are part of the problem. Our why comes from our belief that as human beings, it is our duty to give our resources, knowledge, experience, and time to improve the lives of our brothers and sisters.

Mission

THE What ?

We invest in new businesses and proj- ects that solve very specific problems and sparks self-sustaining income and solutions to the needs of the communi- ties. These businesses and projects adds jobs to where there are none, stimulates the economy where it is struggling, and brings answers to major issues that im- proves the lives of families. Through leadership & consulting expertise, we coach and train individuals to lead and eventually, empower them to run the self-sustaining business or project. We also coach, consult, and advise pre-es- tablished businesses and organizations around the world that are seeking to grow, adapt, and innovate to best impact the villages and cities around them.
